## üìñ About the Project

**Truth Has a Half Life** is a narrative-driven medieval mystery game
where you assume the role of the High Inquisitor of Vaelor.

The Immortal King lies dying under mysterious circumstances. You must
explore fractured memory chambers, collect weighted evidence, and accuse
the correct culprit before time runs out.

But there is a twist.

No matter how well you perform, the King ultimately dies.

The game challenges the illusion of permanence --- not through
exposition, but through mechanics, symbolism, and consequence. The real
victory is not saving the King, but securing justice and guiding the
kingdom toward acceptance and stability.
